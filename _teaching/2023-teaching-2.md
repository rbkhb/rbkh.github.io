I am an Associate Professor of Linguistics at Aarhus University based at the [Interacting Minds Center](https://interactingminds.au.dk/), and founding Co-Director of the [Center for Language Generation and AI](http://clai.au.dk) (CLAI). My research lies at the intersection of linguistic semantics, Natural Language Processing, information theory, and media analytics. As a specialist on applying NLP methods to large scale digital media datasets, I am the PI of the [AUFF Starting Grant](https://auff.au.dk/) project Causal Reasoning and Online Science Skepticism (CROSS) and a work package lead on several large data-driven projects. With [Arthur Hjorth](https://pure.au.dk/portal/da/persons/hermes-arthur-hjorth(b6da5c3f-dc2f-4376-a964-cec167d512e6).html), I am also the co-creator of NLP4All, a digital platform for bringing literacy and conceptual understanding of NLP and machine learning to non-specialists, currently deployed in several AU classrooms and regional high schools. Previously, I was a [Mellon Postdoctoral Fellow at Stanford University](http://mellonfellowship.stanford.edu/) (2015-2018) and taught linguistics as a lecturer at [UCSD](http://ling.ucsd.edu/) (2014-2015). I received my PhD from the [University of Chicago](https://linguistics.uchicago.edu/) in 2015.
